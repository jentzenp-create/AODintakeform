The Rules

How It Works

Just like Super Bowl squares—each square is assigned two digits. After every round, we look at the highest-scoring game. If the last digit of each team’s final score matches your square, you win a box of Girl Scout Cookies delivered to you.
🎲

No basketball knowledge required. Every square has an equal shot at winning—it’s pure luck of the draw.
